Navient makes $1.85 billion settlement amid ongoing lawsuits

Madi Willoughby

Key Trends

Navient Corporation has reached a settlement that requires it to give $1.85 billion in relief to private and federal student loan borrowers, according to Student Loan Planner.

Navient, one of the largest federal student loan services in the U.S., has been involved in several lawsuits since 2017. It has taken advantage of over 66,000 student loan borrowers and 36,587 complaints have been filed against the company in the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau since Dec. 1, 2011.

Most of the complaints were filed in the Northeast, with a total of 10,640 complaints filed since Dec. 1, 2011. The least complaints were in the plains, which had 3,951 complaints filed since Dec. 1, 2011.
