Taylor's Top Performances

Live at the 2019 American Music Awards

This is my favorite Taylor Swift performance of all time because it shows her evolution. She performed this mashup of her iconic music when she won artist of the decade. I turn this on while I'm getting ready to hype myself up. Sometimes I have to force myself not to listen to it so I don't get sick of it.

Cornelia Street (Live From Paris)

Something about this live performances makes me emotional. It's Cornelia Street, a song from Lover that she sang live in Paris just before the pandemic. Hearing this song just on the guitar makes it so much stronger for me than how it is on the actual album.

Clean/Long Live/New Year's Day Live at the Reputation Tour

All of these songs together and acoustic are so amazing. It makes me wish I was there.

All Too Well (10 Minute Version) (Live on Saturday Night Live)

Of course this one deserves a mention. I would like to thank SNL for letting Taylor have 10 minutes on air to perform this iconic version of All Too Well.

All Too Well (Grammys)

Another one of this song for good measure. This performance at the Grammys was so emotional and compelling, I love.
